How To Play

The aim of the game is to rearrange the tiles into numerical order, with the empty space in the bottom right.

This game was a popular handheld toy in the days before Nintendo.

Click on a tile to move it. You can only move a tile if it is touching the empty space.

Tiles will turn green when they are in the correct positions.

You have two minutes to complete the game, which will commence when you make your first move.
